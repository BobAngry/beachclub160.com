Welcome to the Beach Club

The Beach Club Condominium Association is located 1/4 mile from the beach in the heart of Indialantic Florida. The Beach Club is a community of 160 one and two bedroom condominiums, spread between eight residential buildings, all with beautiful lake views. The community has a large pool, spa, laundry facilities, exercise room, and a clubhouse with controlled access and security cameras.
